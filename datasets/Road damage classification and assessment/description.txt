Road damage classification
This dataset contains images of roads with damages.
We classify the damages of roads into 4 categories such as good, poor, satisfactory, very poor according to their extent of damage.
You can use labels to the images by the name given by the directories or by the prefix of name of the image.
You can use these images to classify the roads and also can be used for further assesment.
